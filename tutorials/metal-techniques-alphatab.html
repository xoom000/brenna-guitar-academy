üõù Slides (sl)

üî® Hammer-ons (h)

Strike the string with your fretting finger to sound a note without picking. Creates fluid legato playing.

üõù Slides Technique

Slides create smooth transitions between notes. Keep pressure on the string and slide your finger along the fretboard. You'll see this notated as 3sl5 (slide from 3rd to 5th fret).

üî® Hammer-ons Technique

Hammer-ons let you play notes without picking every single one. Pick the first note, then "hammer" your finger down on the next fret. Notated as 0h2 (hammer from open to 2nd fret).

ü™ù Pull-offs Technique

Pull-offs are the opposite of hammer-ons. Start with both fingers down, pick the higher note, then pull your finger off to sound the lower note. Notated as 3p0 (pull off from 3rd fret to open).
