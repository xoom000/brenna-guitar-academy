Classical vs Rock Position

For metal and rock guitar, most players use the casual position - guitar resting on your right leg, neck angled slightly upward. This gives you better access to higher frets for those sick solos!

Fretting Technique

Press down just behind the fret wire (not on top of it). Use your fingertips, and press firmly enough to get a clear sound without buzzing.

What Are Power Chords?

Power chords use just 2-3 strings and create that massive, aggressive sound you hear in metal. They're easier than full chords and sound way more badass!

The Secret to Metal Rhythm

Palm muting is when you lightly rest the side of your picking hand on the strings near the bridge. This creates that tight, percussive sound that makes metal so heavy.
